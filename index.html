About me

My name is Patrick Gould and I am a native of the Greater Boston Area. I have spent my the majority of my life in and around city of Boston and I'm proud to call it home. At the age of 23 years old I am currently a Full Stack Web Developer and graduate of the UNH Coding Bootcamp. I absolutely love coding and pretty much everything tech related. Since I was a child I have aspired to work with computers and through hard work and dedication I have finally achieved that goal.
